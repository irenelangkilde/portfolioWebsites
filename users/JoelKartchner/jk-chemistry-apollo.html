Laser Ablation RF Filters Flex Cables

Laser fabrication for RF hardware that has to perform in the real world.

In BYU’s Laser Ablation Lab, I developed fabrication workflows for RF metal mesh filters and microscopic flexible cables using direct-write laser ablation. The work demanded both process precision and device-level thinking—connecting fabrication choices to RF behavior, manufacturability, and measurement.

  • Developed a laser ablation tool for creating RF metal mesh filters and micro-scale flexible cables.
  • Worked across fabrication, RF design concerns, and research validation in a publication-backed environment.
  • Contributed to the 2024 IETC paper on high-density flex cable fabrication using direct-write laser ablation.
