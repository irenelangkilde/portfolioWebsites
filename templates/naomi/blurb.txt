This resume and portfolio website is built for biology graduates who want employers to see more than a list of classes. It gives you a clear, professional space to showcase lab experience, fieldwork, research projects, and scientific writing in context. Instead of vague bullet points, you can demonstrate how you collect data, follow protocols, analyze results, and communicate findings. The layout emphasizes precision, documentation, and evidence—qualities hiring managers look for in research, healthcare, environmental, and biotech roles. Whether you’re applying to labs, conservation organizations, or science-adjacent positions, the site helps translate academic training into real-world capability. It makes your work easy to review, easy to trust, and easy to remember. This is how you turn scientific training into hiring confidence.
