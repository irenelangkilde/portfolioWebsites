😊 Our Happiness Guarantee

Every product and service sold by Irene's Webworks is backed by a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not satisfied with what you received, contact us and we will work with you — through revisions, replacements, or a full refund — until the situation is resolved fairly.

We are a small business built on trust and word-of-mouth. Your satisfaction matters more to us than protecting any single transaction.

🎁 Gift Codes & Gift Purchases

Gift codes are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. If a gift code is purchased and never redeemed, a refund is available within 90 days of purchase.

Once a gift code has been redeemed and work has begun, the standard portfolio website refund terms above apply to the recipient's project.

💬 Bought a gift for someone who ended up not needing it? Email us — we'll work something out. We're reasonable people.

📬 How to Request a Refund

We keep this simple on purpose. No forms, no ticket queues, no automated responses. Just email Irene directly:

  1. Email irene@irenes-ventures.com with your name, the product or service you purchased, and a brief description of the issue.
  2. We will respond within 2 business days — usually much sooner.
  3. We'll discuss the situation and agree on the right resolution: revision, replacement, partial refund, or full refund.
  4. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method.
💡 The more detail you share about what didn't meet your expectations, the faster we can find a resolution. Screenshots, examples, and specific feedback are all welcome.
