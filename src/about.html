Irene Langkilde

Founder · Irene's Webworks

I started Irene's Webworks because I believe the benefits of a polished web presence shouldn't be a luxury reserved for the wealthy or technically inclined. Every job-seeker deserves a website that makes employers stop scrolling.

My background spans web development, AI engineering, and natural language processing. Irene's Webworks uses AI to do the heavy lifting: pulling your resume details, matching your field's aesthetic, and generating a site that looks like a senior designer built it — in minutes, not weeks, at a fraction of the cost of a freelance developer or a laborious do-it-yourself approach.

My mission is straightforward: democratize the web. Small businesses, new graduates, career-changers — everyone should be able to put their best foot forward online without needing to spend thousands or learn to code. In recent weeks you would have found me deep in color theory, obsessing over typography, or helping someone figure out how to tell their professional story in a way that actually lands.

When I'm not building tools that level the playing field, I'm spending time with my 5 children, 10 siblings, 50 nieces and nephews, and/or 35 first-cousins and their families.

Have a question, a project idea, or just want to say hello? irene@irenes-ventures.com
