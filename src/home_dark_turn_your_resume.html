Why a portfolio website works better than a resume alone

Hiring managers often scan quickly. A personal website gives them a fuller picture of what you’ve done, what you can do, and what makes you memorable.

Resume Alone

  • Compressed into a page or two
  • Limited room for projects and proof
  • Often looks similar to everyone else
  • Harder to express range and depth

Portfolio Website

  • Can expand to fit your real accomplishments
  • Lets you show projects, demos, presentations, and publications
  • Creates a more distinctive first impression
  • Gives recruiters a stronger reason to remember you

Stand out visually

Instead of blending into a stack of résumés, you give viewers a clearer and more engaging presentation of your experience.

Show real proof

Add projects, research, code, presentations, demos, and other concrete evidence of your strengths.

Stay editable

Update your site as you gain internships, publish work, change goals, or tailor your applications.
