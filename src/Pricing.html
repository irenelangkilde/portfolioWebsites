How It Works
What is a credit? A credit = one chunk of AI processing. 3 credits power one full pipeline pass; revisions cost 0–3 depending on scope.
Download vs. deploy? A download saves the site to your computer. A deploy publishes it live to the internet.
Why host with Irene's Webworks vs self-host? Irene's Webworks includes global content delivery, fast load times, HTTPS security, and strong uptime performance.
Can I mix plans and add-ons? Yes — choose a plan above, then set quantities in the add-ons section and buy them separately.
What if I need more credits later? Buy Extra Credits any time from this page. Graduate subscribers pay $5/credit; Prime subscribers pay $4/credit.
