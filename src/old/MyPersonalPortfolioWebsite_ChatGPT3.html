        Tips:
        
      
          
    1. Don’t aim for perfection on the first iteration. Draft fast, refine later.
      2. 
          
    2. Show, don’t just tell: add proof (projects, metrics, links, screenshots, outcomes).
      3. 
          
    3. Lead with a 1-line headline: target role + specialty + value.
      4. 
          
    4. Quantify impact wherever possible (%, $, time saved, volume, accuracy, ratings).
      5. 
          
    5. Write for skimmers: short sections, strong headings, bullets, whitespace.
      6. 
          
    6. Tailor to the job ad: mirror keywords (truthfully) and map evidence to requirements.
      7. 
          
    7. Make contact frictionless: email + LinkedIn visible, clear call-to-action.
