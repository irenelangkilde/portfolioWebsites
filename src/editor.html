Keep your color edits?

A new portfolio just arrived in the editor. You have unsaved color changes in the workbench. What should we do?

Keep my edits: the workbench panel keeps your substitutes. Your overrides may not visibly apply if the new template uses a different color structure.
Discard and use new colors: the workbench resets to the new template's palette.
