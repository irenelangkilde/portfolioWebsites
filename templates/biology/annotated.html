Publications & Presentations

Academic contributions and conference presentations

Effects of Climate Change on Native Plant Phenology
Pearson, H., Johnson, R., & Smith, K. | Journal of Ecology | 2025
Examined shifts in flowering times of native plant species over a 20-year period in response to changing temperature patterns. Found significant correlations between early spring warming and advanced flowering dates.
CRISPR-Cas9 Optimization in Arabidopsis thaliana
Pearson, H. & Chen, L. | Plant Molecular Biology Reports | 2024
Developed improved protocols for CRISPR-Cas9 gene editing in model plant species. Achieved 85% editing efficiency with reduced off-target effects through optimized guide RNA design.
Soil Microbiome Analysis in Restored Wetlands
Presented at Ecological Society of America Annual Meeting | 2024
Poster presentation analyzing bacterial community composition in restored wetland ecosystems. Identified key indicator species associated with successful wetland restoration outcomes.
